# How to Buy a Phone Number

Phone numbers are how your superU Voice AI agents connect with real people. Once you buy a number, you can attach it to any agent for inbound or outbound campaigns.

### Why You Need a Number

Every live call made through superU AI runs through a verified phone number. Buying your own number ensures your calls look professional, helps track campaigns, and allows inbound calls to reach your agent directly. You can choose local or international numbers depending on where your customers are.

## Step 1: Login and Buy a Number

Log in to your superU workspace and go to the **Phone Numbers** tab from the left menu.

![image 14](/platform-guide/images/image%2014.png)

Click **"Buy New Number"** after choosing a country or region. You'll see a list of available numbers.

![image 15](/platform-guide/images/image%2015.png)

Pick the area that matches your customer base. We offer phone numbers of major countries. For example, if you serve clients in the US, choose a US number for a US-based caller ID.

## Step 2: Review and Confirm

Once you select a number, review the details and click **Buy Number**. The number will be added to your workspace instantly.

You'll now see your purchased number listed under **Phone Numbers** with its status set to **Active**.

# Start an Outbound Campaign

Outbound campaigns help you reach your audience directly: to qualify leads, follow up with sign-ups, share updates, or announce offers. With superU AI, all of this happens automatically, at scale, while maintaining a natural, human-like tone.

## Build Your Agent

Choose how you want to build your agent. You can start from scratch, let AI create one from your brief, or pick a ready-made template to get started quickly.

![Screenshot 2025-11-08 135454](/platform-guide/images/Screenshot_2025-11-08_135454.png)

If you choose to use our templates, you'll see an assorted set of templates. Select one which suits you.

![image](/platform-guide/images/image.png)

Just click on 'Create Agent' to see your agent created on your dashboard.

![image 16](/platform-guide/images/image%2016.png)

If you would rather create your agent using our AI's help, describe the kind of agent you want to build and the assistant will create a ready-to-edit draft. Edit as per your wishes and click 'Publish'.

## Upload Your Audience

Head to the **Audience** tab.

![image 18](/platform-guide/images/image%2018.png)

Upload your CSV with customer names and phone numbers.

![image 19](/platform-guide/images/image%2019.png)

Map CSV columns and check if you have any validation errors. Create the audience when it looks right.

## Assign a Phone Number

Open the **Phone Numbers** tab in your dashboard. Click **Buy New Number**, pick your preferred country or region, and confirm. Once purchased, assign that number to your outbound agent.

![image 20](/platform-guide/images/image%2020.png)

This will be the caller ID shown when your agent makes calls.

## Set Up Your Campaign

Go to **Campaigns â†’ Create Campaign**.

![image 21](/platform-guide/images/image%2021.png)

Enter a campaign name and select your outbound agent.

Choose your audience list and the phone number to use. Pick a date, time, and number of concurrent calls. Review everything and click **Create Campaign** to launch.

![Gemini Generated Image](/platform-guide/images/Gemini_Generated_Image_4q3xy04q3xy04q3x.png)

Once your campaign begins, your agent will automatically start calling contacts. That's it! Your superU AI agent is now live making outbound calls.

# Link Phone Number to an Agent for Inbound

Inbound campaigns help your business handle incoming calls smoothly. With superU AI, your agent can greet callers, answer questions, route requests, or record feedback, all in real time.

## Build Your Agent

Choose how you want to build your agent. You can start from scratch, let AI create one from your brief, or pick a ready-made template to get started quickly.

![Screenshot 2025-11-08 135454](/platform-guide/images/Screenshot_2025-11-08_135454.png)

If you choose to use our templates, you'll see an assorted set of templates. Select one which suits you.

![image](/platform-guide/images/image.png)

Just click on 'Create Agent' to see your agent created on your dashboard.

![image 1](/platform-guide/images/image%201.png)

After that step, your agent will be visible on your dashboard. Once you click on it, you can try 'Test AI Agent' to hear your agent.

![image 2](/platform-guide/images/image%202.png)

If you would rather create your agent using our AI's help, describe the kind of agent you want to build. The assistant will turn your idea into a ready-to-edit draft. Edit as per your wishes and click 'Publish'.

![image 3](/platform-guide/images/image%203.png)

Browse the **Voice Library** from the sidebar, filter by provider, gender, or language, and play a few samples to find one that matches your agent's tone and personality.

![image 5](/platform-guide/images/image%205.png)

## Buy a Phone Number

Go to the **Phone Numbers** tab. Click **Buy New Number**, choose a region or country, and confirm the purchase. Once added, the number appears in your list and is ready to be connected.

![image 22](/platform-guide/images/image%2022.png)

## Connect Your Agent to an Inbound Campaign

Go to the **Campaign** tab in your superU dashboard.

![image 23](/platform-guide/images/image%2023.png)

Click **Inbound Campaigns** and create a new inbound campaign. Select the **phone number** you've purchased or added earlier.

Under **Assigned Agent**, choose the inbound agent you created.

![image 24](/platform-guide/images/image%2024.png)

Once assigned, the campaign status will change to **Active**, and your agent will be ready to **receive live incoming calls**.

![image 25](/platform-guide/images/image%2025.png)

## Test Your Inbound Flow

Call the assigned number from your phone. Listen to how your agent greets and interacts. If needed, update your greeting, voice, or logic directly in the dashboard. Test again until you're satisfied with the experience.

That's it! Your superU AI agent is now live and ready to handle incoming calls 24/7.
